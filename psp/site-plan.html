Content

Home page

Welcome

Hello and welcome to my professional portfolio. My name is Noah Evans, and I am a dedicated professional with a background in sales and a current pursuit of a degree in Software Engineering at Brigham Young University - Idaho.

About Me

This site showcases my sales achievements and technical skills acquired through my academic and professional journey. With a solid foundation in sales and a burgeoning expertise in software engineering, I am equipped to deliver innovative solutions and drive success.

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to showcase my sales achievements and newly acquired IT skills to potential employers and recruiters.

Audience

The intended audience includes potential employers in the sales industry, recruiters, and professional connections interested in my sales experience and technical skills.

Why Visit

People will come to my site to learn about my professional journey, accomplishments, and skills. My portfolio provides a comprehensive overview of my sales achievements, technical expertise, and leadership abilities, which potential employers and recruiters will find valuable.

Visitor Questions

Visitors will expect my site to answer questions about my career history, the specific projects I have worked on, and the results I have achieved. They will want to see detailed descriptions of my roles, responsibilities, and the impact I have made in each position.

Visitor Activities

Visitors may want to engage in activities such as viewing my resume, reading testimonials from colleagues, and exploring case studies of successful projects. They may also want to contact me for potential job opportunities or professional networking.

Required Information

To complete any actions on my site, visitors will need access to my contact information, detailed descriptions of my professional experiences, and links to my LinkedIn profile and other professional networks.

Why Choose My Site

Visitors will come to my site instead of another to get this information because it is tailored to highlight my unique combination of sales and technical skills, along with my proven track record of success in the solar energy industry. My portfolio is designed to be visually appealing, easy to navigate, and informative, ensuring that potential employers and recruiters can quickly find the information they need.

My quality computer skills and efficiency with CRM systems allowed me to confidently communicate and relate with people from diverse backgrounds.
